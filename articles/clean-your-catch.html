Clean Your Catch

Unless your old school and just use your tailgate or are prepared enough to bring a portable fish cleaning table, you might want to use one of these publicaly available facilities.

Port A Municipal Harbor

Right across the parking lot from the boat ramp near the public restrooms, there is a fish cleaning house. Quite often there is a gentleman there that will clean your fish. He doesn’t ask for money, but everyone tips him cash, beer, or lets him keep some fish. He does a great job.

Billings Bait Stand

Like most, they prefer that you buy something, and they do have bait and fishing supplies, so you might start and end there to use their fish cleaning tables. There is plenty of space for everyone here.

Clems Marina

Clems also has a nice fishing pier (for a fee), but also have a fish cleaning station. They also sell snacks, drinks, bait and fishing supplies.

Marker 37

Has boat ramps, food nearby, snacks, drinks, some fishing merch, and bait. The fish cleaning station is on the exit of their marina on the channel side across from their fishing pier. Their fishing pier has great covered spaces you can “rent” for a fee. They also have fish cleaning service.
