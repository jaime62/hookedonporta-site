Dialing in Your First Outing

Start with a rising or falling tide. Plant your rod holder in firm sand angled slightly toward the water. Cast beyond the first bar, set your drag just tight enough to keep from free-spooling, and watch the tip. If the current sweeps your rig, step up your sinker weight. If bait comes back untouched, move down the beach or switch baits.

And remember: distance helps, but presentation wins. Fresh bait, sharp hooks, and clean knots beat hero casts every time.
